Rules

Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking a vacant cell with their respective symbols, usually "X" and "O," aiming to create a line of three of their symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game begins with an empty grid, and each player alternates placing their symbol in an empty cell. The first player to achieve a winning line or fill the entire grid without a winner results in a draw. The objective is to strategically place symbols to block the opponent and set up potential winning moves, making it a game of skill and strategy.
